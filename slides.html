what about art?


art is a game played with language


languages are collections of memes

a meme is the smallest possible unit of language that carries a meaning. this meaning is aquired as the meme is used and reused, and the meaning may change over time.

namespace for memes

* [meme title]/[username]/[timestamp]
::--> That's specific enough.
* [meme title]/[username]
::--> you get that user's most recent version
* [meme title]
::--> a version compiled from all edit differences ranked by popularity

what's the diff?

  • no single user may ever fully control the content of a meme
  • to provide the joy authorship, users may access and share their personal contributions to the meme
